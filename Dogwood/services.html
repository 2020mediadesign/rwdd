What We Do?

We offer full landscape and irrigation installation services to both residential and commercial customers and with more than 35 years in the industry there isn't anything we can't do.

Click on our projects page to see some of our sprinkler and irrigation systems in action and if you are pleased with what you see, please give us a call today to setup a consultation with one of our specialists. The difference between Dogwood landscaping and other Triangle area landscape companies is a combination of our experience, knowledge, attention to detail and professionalism. We are dedicated to providing exceptional products and services at the lowest price possible. So the next time you are seeking out commercial irrigation, residential irrigation or maybe just a new look for your yard, look no further than Dogwood Landscape Design.
