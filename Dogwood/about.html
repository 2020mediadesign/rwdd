About Us

About Us!

Dogwood Landscaping; Design, Inc. has serviced both Commercial and Residential builders in the Triangle area for the past 18 years. Began in 1994, when Greg Todd and David Munn decided to form a partnership to bring together their expertise and professionalism in irrigation and horticulture. Through the Triangle areas boom times and even today the company has remained and continues to serve the area with that same level of expertise and professionalism.

Our area of expertise has grown over the years so that we can offer our customers many services other companys just can't offer. From commercial retention ponds to a water feature in in a back yard, tree relocation, irrigation, and beyond try Dogwood Landscape Design.
