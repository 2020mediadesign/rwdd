About me.....

I enjoy learning and the pursuit of greater understanding. That pursuit has taken me around the world in a decade long adventure that taught one very valuable lesson. That lesson was that it is not the destination that holds enlightenment but rather the journey itself.

Upon my return home I decided to revisit a stalled adventure that began in 1986 when I got my first computer. I have been utterly obsessed with them since that day and have gone to great lengths to keep them in my life no matter where I was.

Late in 2006 I had an oppertunity to visit MIT and I was inspired greatly. But it wasn't until I met a very like minded individual with a similar passion that I would actually enroll in school and begin to see this journey to its end.

In 2007 I met my a wonderful person and knew almost instantly that I wanted to spent a considerable amount of time with her. In 2008 we began a journey so massive in its undertaking that it puts all other journeys, trips, vendettas, sojourns, voyages, expeditions, jaunts, and missions to shame. We both enrolled in school full time and started a family amidst the full economic collapse of our country.

 Here we are in 2012 and it seems we are coming to the end of our journey. The pieces of paper our scool will hand us in a few months will mean little to us as the journey to this point in time has changed us so much. Our daughter is now almost 3 years old, the country is in repair, I am immersed in ever changing technology and can see the light at the end of the tunnel.

 I don't know what the next journey will be but I have a feeling it will a 3 year old little girl in the drivers seat.
